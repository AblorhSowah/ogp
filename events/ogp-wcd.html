World Oceans Day Celebrations 2023

Event

The Ocean Governance Project at the University of Ghana School of Law invites you to a Stakeholders’ Consultative Forum on the Ocean Governance Study commissioned by the Division of Ocean Affairs and Law of the Sea (DOALOS) of the United Nations.
This event will be preceded by two brief ceremonies to commemorate the 40th Anniversary of the signing of the United Nations Convention for the Law of the Sea (UNCLOS), 1982 and the World Oceans Day which falls on 8th June 2023.
The Forum is funded by the Norwegian Agency for Development Cooperation (NORAD), under the Ocean Governance Project run by the University of Ghana School of Law in collaboration with the Department of Marine and Fisheries Sciences at the University of Ghana.

Date: Thursday, June 8, 2023
Time: 09:00 to 14:00
Venue: University of Ghana School of Law

Mode: Hybrid
